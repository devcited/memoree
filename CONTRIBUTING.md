# Contributing to Memoree

Issues, bug reports, design discussion, and documentation feedback are welcome.

## Code and documentation contributions

Memoree is not currently accepting pull requests or other contributed code or documentation. Please do not submit material for inclusion in the repository until a contributor agreement has been published and accepted.

This boundary preserves the Licensor's ability to maintain the project's source-available and commercial licensing models. Opening an issue or participating in a discussion does not transfer ownership of your material or authorize its incorporation into Memoree.

The contribution policy will be updated before any external contribution is accepted or merged.

## Security reports

Do not report undisclosed vulnerabilities in a public issue. Follow [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) and email [security@devcited.cc](mailto:security@devcited.cc).

For permission to sell Memoree or offer a product or service whose value derives entirely or substantially from Memoree, contact Valeriy Efimenko at [licensing@memoree.dev](mailto:licensing@memoree.dev).
